Detox: Support Your Body’s Natural Cleansing

Your body detoxes naturally through liver, kidneys, lungs, skin, and gut. This guide explains what works, what to avoid, and includes a 7-day vegetarian-friendly plan.

Quick Summary

  • Works: Hydration, fiber, protein, sleep, exercise, stress management.
  • Use with care: Herbal supplements—consult a doctor.
  • Avoid: Extreme cleanses, unregulated detox products.

How Natural Detox Works

  • Liver: Converts toxins for excretion.
  • Kidneys: Filter blood and balance fluids.
  • Gut: Fiber supports microbiome and waste removal.
  • Skin & Lungs: Sweat and breath remove small amounts.
  • Sleep: Brain clears metabolic by-products during deep sleep.

7-Day Vegetarian-Friendly Plan

Balanced meals with Indian context: oats upma, ragi dosa, dal, khichdi, sprouts chaat, paneer/tofu dishes.

  • Day 1: Add flax seeds to breakfast; brisk walk.
  • Day 2: Include greens in meals; mobility exercises.
  • Day 3: Focus on protein; sleep by 11 pm.
  • Day 4: Fermented foods like curd/buttermilk.
  • Day 5: Colorful veggies and fruits.
  • Day 6: Reduce ultra-processed foods.
  • Day 7: Light dinner; yoga and digital detox.

FAQs

  • Do I need detox products? No, prioritize whole foods and sleep.
  • Is fasting required? Not necessary; gentle time-restricted eating is okay.
  • How much water? 2–3 L/day spread out.
